Multi-Agent System for Idram

AI Agents for Contact Center First Line

Idram is one of the leading Armenian fintech platforms providing digital wallet and banking services.

This project became one of the first successful large-scale Conversational AI deployments in the Armenian language.

The system was designed as a multi-agent AI architecture for the first line of the contact center, focused on handling service outages and customer communication in real time.

A dedicated control panel was developed for bank employees, allowing operators to activate AI agents manually during incidents affecting more than 10 banking and payment services.

The AI system automates communication workflows during outages and significantly reduces the operational load on the contact center.

Agent Architecture

Agent 1
Handles inbound customer calls, informs users about ongoing service disruptions, explains the nature of the issue, and provides real-time status updates.

Agent 2
Logs and structures all incoming calls and incident-related interactions during the outage period.

Agent 3
Automatically launches outbound calls to notify customers once services are restored.

Key Metrics

| Metric | Value | Business Impact |
|---|---|---|
| Automation Rate | More than 80% of requests processed automatically | Most incidents resolved without transfer to human operators |
| Outage Response Speed | Real-time communication | Customers instantly informed about service disruptions |
| Post-Incident Communication | 100% automated outbound notifications | Higher transparency and stronger customer trust |

Technical Challenge

One of the key technical challenges of the project was the low accuracy of Armenian automatic speech recognition systems.

The Armenian language had a high Word Error Rate (WER), meaning speech recognition engines frequently misunderstood words, phrases, and customer intent during phone conversations.

Solution

To compensate for speech recognition limitations, a proprietary Natural Language Understanding (NLU) classifier and custom conversational platform logic were developed.

This approach enabled the system to:
- correctly detect customer intent,
- maintain stable dialogue behavior,
- provide accurate responses,
- and operate reliably despite imperfect speech recognition quality.

The solution supports real-time interaction through:

The system operates on top of a centralized Smart Knowledge Base that consolidates regulatory information, internal procedures, operational data, and citizen-facing guidance into a unified AI-accessible environment.
